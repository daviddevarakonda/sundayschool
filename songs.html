3.Colours colours five pretty colours

Colours colours five pretty colours (2)
Colours tell a story, listen carefully
1. Gold Colour tells the glory of Heaven
Where Jesus is building mansions for his children
2. Black Colour shows the sin of man
All have sinned and no difference
3. Red Colour tells of blood He shed
Which cleanses us from every sin
4. White colour tells of Purity
Wash your heart and be His child
5. Green shows growth grow like a tree
Grow in His grace and Knowledge
